ABOUT

My interest in design started at the age of around twelve years old. Back then, I read a lot of comics and played games, and after a while I started creating and drawing my own comics and gaming characters.

My interest in design grew stronger and stronger during that period in my life. When I was seventeen years old I started studying “Graphic Design” at Grafisch Lyceum Rotterdam, and after four enjoyable years, I finally graduated and received my diploma. In those four years we had multiple courses in Web Design (HTML & CSS), and although it was just the basics, it was enough for me to say that I wanted to become a Web Designer, and therefore I decided to do one extra year of studying after having graduated. This extra year was specialised in “Interactive Design”, whereby I learned more and more about Web Design and HTML & CSS, receiving my second diploma one year later.

Even after that extra year of studies, I wanted to learn more. This is why I started studying “Creative Media and Game Technologies” at Hogeschool Rotterdam, and a whole new world opened up for me. I learnt more than only simple HTML & CSS, having created my own websites in PHP, my own games in Typescript, played around in frameworks like Laravel, worked on projects to make airports around the world more smart and stepped into the world of IOT. My education gave me the freedom to search for what I wanted to be, and I want to be an Front-End Developer.

In addition, I’ve learned to work in sprints and the use of user stories and to appreciate the use of brainstorms, concepts, prototyping and testing.

Right now, I’m a second year student and looking for an internship as a Front-End Developer. I have basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, Javascript, Typescript and PHP, and I’m eager to learn new techniques, frameworks, and programming languages.

GrouPay

Project

This is a project I started with a friend, whereby we wanted to create a mobile app to facilitate group payments in the catering industry. The idea is, when you go out for a drink with people, to create a group where everyone can individually order consumptions. The application will be linked with the company where you are consuming food and beverages. At all times there will be an overview of what everyone ordered and what everyone will have to pay. When its time to pay, the money will be written of their bank accounts individually and the payment will be done by smartphone. After a lot of brainstorming, research, interviews, designs and pitches we unfortunately cancelled this project due to a lack of time.

I created all designs for the app in Adobe XD.
